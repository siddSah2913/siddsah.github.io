About Me

Who I Am

I'm Siddhant Sah, a first-year student pursuing a BCS in Artificial Intelligence at The British College, Kathmandu, affiliated with the University of the West of England (UWE Bristol).

I grew up in Nepal with a genuine curiosity about how technology works and why it matters. Seeing the gap between what technology can do and how little of it reaches people around me shaped a clear goal — to understand AI deeply enough to actually build useful things with it.

Right now I'm focused on getting the fundamentals right: programming, mathematics, and the building blocks of intelligent systems. I believe strong foundations are what separate people who use AI from people who build it.

✦ Semester 1 · Hardware Project
Vehicle Parking Management System
Arduino · Embedded Systems · Semester 1, BCS AI — The British College

Designed and built a microcontroller-based parking management system using Arduino as part of the Semester 1 embedded systems module. The system monitors parking slot availability in real time and communicates status through LEDs (green for available, red for occupied) and a buzzer alert triggered when capacity is reached. The project involved circuit design, sensor integration, and writing control logic in C — reinforcing low-level programming concepts alongside practical electronics skills acquired during the semester.
